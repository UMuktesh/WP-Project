FAQ


Q: What comes under large events?
A: All the events that are on a large scale,i.e., the number of guests are more than 1000 or the size of the ground or hall in which the event is being held is large.

Q: What are the rates to organize these events?
A: The rates may vary according to the requirement, number of guests and the size of the ground or hall in which the event is being held.

Q: How can I book an event?
A: The event can be booked in the book section on the home page.

Q: What would be the minimum charge for the event?
A: The minimum charges of all the events are given in the rates section in the home page.

Q: How am I supposed to guess what the rates would be?
A: The rates would be told to you within 4 working days after you book your event by either E-mail or call.

Q: What are the cancelation policies?
A: You can cancel your event for free before 4 days of your event. If you however cancel it after that you will only recieve 50% of the amount.
